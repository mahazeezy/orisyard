📋 Policies

Order Policies

Please read before placing your order. We want everything to go smoothly for you!

💰
Deposit for Cakes
50% non-refundable deposit required to secure a cake order. Remaining balance due 2 days before pickup date.
📦
Shipping Orders
Items that need to be shipped must be paid in full to secure the order. No exceptions.
🎨
Custom Designs
Custom designs should be ordered 2–3 weeks in advance to ensure all decorations arrive on time.
🍰
One Flavor Per Product
Only one cake flavor per product. Please choose your flavor when placing your order.
📅
Order in Advance
Orders must be placed at least 1 week before pickup date. Orders placed after will be considered a rush order — contact for more info.
💳
Payment Methods
We accept Apple Pay and Cash App only.
